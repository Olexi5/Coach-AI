Why are educational losses important?

The war and quarantine have caused significant educational losses, as schoolchildren and students have been forced to miss classes. In Ukraine, these losses are estimated at $5.5 billion. As a result, students risk losing up to $1,400 in annual income, and over their lifetime, this amount could reach $25,000. If schools around the world are closed for five months, the cost of educational losses will be $10 trillion.
