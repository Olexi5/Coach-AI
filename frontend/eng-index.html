Educational AI tutor in your browser

Studying can be quite challenging these days. But now there is an AI tutor to help you understand any topic, improve your knowledge of subjects, and make the learning process exciting.

Why educational loss matters

The war and pandemic have caused significant educational losses, as schoolchildren and students have been forced to miss classes. In Ukraine, these losses are estimated at $5.5 billion. As a result, students risk losing up to $1,400 in annual income, and over their lifetime, this amount could reach $25,000 . If schools around the world are closed for five months , the cost of educational losses will be $10 trillion.

What the bot can do

Our AI tutor is designed to help you overcome gaps in your learning. It analyses your knowledge level, identifies your strengths and weaknesses, and creates a personalised learning plan just for you. Everything is presented in a convenient way, including videos, quizzes, interactive tasks, and game elements to make learning fun and engaging. The bot explains everything in simple language, so you can understand it easily. It explains your mistakes, suggests exercises to correct them, and regularly shows your progress so you can evaluate your achievements and understand what you need to work on next.

  • For teenagers (12-17):
